Post-A-Letter Social Activity Club

Imagine a day when every personal email you receive is in the form of a piece of mail, in envelopes of different sizes, papers of different colours and textures, handwriting of varying degrees of legibility. Wouldn't that be pretty nice for a change?

Portland

Back in May, Pal-Sac hosted a pop-up post office during Open Engagement, a conference on socially-engaged art, in Portland, Oregon. The post office featured mini postal boxes, one for each conference-goer, and a writing station. Special thanks to Mirador Community Store, Red Bird Studio, Land Gallery, Present Perfect, Publication Studio, and Paper Pusher (Toronto) for generous donations!
